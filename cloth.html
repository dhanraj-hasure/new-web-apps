Our Heritage

For over four decades, Avani Silks has been the custodian of Maharashtra's rich textile heritage. What began as a small family venture in 1985 has blossomed into a celebrated destination for authentic handwoven sarees and traditional ethnic wear.

We work directly with master weavers from Yeola, Paithan, and surrounding villages, ensuring fair wages and preserving age-old weaving techniques that have been passed down through generations. Every piece in our collection tells a story of dedication, artistry, and cultural pride.

Handloom Weaving

Zari Work
