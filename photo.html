Hello, I am Arjun Veda.

Photography, for me, is about preserving the soul of the moment. Born and raised in the colorful landscapes of India, I have always been fascinated by the vibrancy of our traditions and the raw emotions of our people.

With over 8 years of experience shooting across Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Bangalore, I specialize in blending traditional Indian aesthetics with a modern, cinematic documentary style.

Whether it's the chaotic joy of a Baraat, the tearful goodbye of a Vidaai, or the innocent laughter of a child's first birthday, I am there to capture it all.
